Quick guide to reach IIT Bombay campus

- Powai is an eastern suburb in North-Eastern part of Mumbai.
- Here is how you can reach IIT Bombay from various important stations.

  • From Airport
  • From Dadar Station
  • From Thane Station
  • From Bandra Terminus
  • From Lokmanya Tilak Terminus
  • From Mumbai Central
  • From Chatrapati Shivaji Terminus
  • Transit Details: From Airport

    Convenient way to reach IITB from airport is to BEST buses running on route no. 382 or 409. Alternatively you may take an auto directly to IIT which would cost you around 90 rs. (depending on traffic) or a taxi which would cost around 100 Rs.
