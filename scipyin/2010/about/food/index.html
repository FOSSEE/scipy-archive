Lunch will be provided on all days. Tea/coffee and biscuits will be provided twice a day on all days. Participants will have to make their own arrangements for breakfast and dinner. They can inform the organizers at the registration desk on Day 1 of the conference if opting to eat any meals at the IIITH messes/canteens. Informing us in prior will ensure availability of food at these places. IIITH offers highly subsidized meals at the rate of Rs.20 for breakfast and Rs.30 for dinner.
